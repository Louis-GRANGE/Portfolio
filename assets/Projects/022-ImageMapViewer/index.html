Image Map Viewer

A flutter application allows you to view your pictures on an interactive map with various features such as:
  • Pinning photos to specific locations on the map
  • Filtering photos by date or location
  • Zooming and panning to explore different areas
  • Viewing photo details
  • Duration of stay in the country
  • Offline map mode to access to the app without an internet connection
