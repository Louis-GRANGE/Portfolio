The Phoenix

"The Phoenix" is a 2D game created with Unity, where the player controls a Phoenix who must protect and feed its chicks while surviving the dangers posed by enemies like cats and hunters. The game has two levels, each presenting a unique set of challenges as the Phoenix tries to keep its offspring safe and thrive in a hostile environment.
