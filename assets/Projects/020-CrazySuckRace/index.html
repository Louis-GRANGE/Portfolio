CrazySuckRace

CrazySuckRace is an exciting multiplayer game built with Unreal Engine, blending the fast-paced action of Ultimate Chicken Horse with the chaotic fun of You Suck At Parking. The game features three distinct phases:

1. Selection Phase: Choose from a variety of map elements, such as road sections, obstacles, checkpoints, and boosts.
2. Placement Phase: Once everyone has made their selections, strategically place your chosen items on the map to create the ultimate racecourse.
3. Race Phase: After the map is set, it's time to race! Speed through the course, dodge obstacles, and be the first to reach the finish line!

Can you outsmart and outdrive your opponents to claim victory?
