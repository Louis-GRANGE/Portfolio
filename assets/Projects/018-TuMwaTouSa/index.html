TuMwaTouSa

Rogue-Like Game featuring a dynamic map generator and a robust save system. The objective is simple yet intense: survive as long as possible while defeating waves of enemies. Every playthrough offers a unique experience with procedurally generated maps and increasing challenges, testing both your skills and strategy.

My Duties:

  • Programmed enemy AI behaviors and balancing.
  • Developed player controller, including weapon and object interactions.
