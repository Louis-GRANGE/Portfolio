Human Fall Escape

"Human Fall Escape" is another immersive Escape Game developed in Unity3D. In this adventure, the player must navigate through a challenging castle environment, solving riddles to escape and reach a train to complete the game. Every step tests both your wit and your ability to think strategically under pressure.
