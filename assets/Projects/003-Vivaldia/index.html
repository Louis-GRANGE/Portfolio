Vivaldia

"Vivaldia" is a 2D game developed with Unity, where the player embarks on a quest to recover the four seasonal objects to restore balance to the world. The character must explore diverse environments, solve intricate puzzles, engage in combat, and remain vigilant against enemies to complete the mission and bring back the four seasons.

My Duties:

  • Programmed the dungeon puzzles to challenge players and enhance exploration.
  • Developed the core mechanics for each seasonal object and their unique abilities.
