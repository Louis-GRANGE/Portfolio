Fallen

"Fallen" is an interactive tutorial game where players follow the instructions of a pangolin guide to navigate through challenges. Developed as part of a team of 7, the game combines programming, modeling, and design elements. The focus is on a smooth player experience enhanced with intuitive controls and immersive visuals.
